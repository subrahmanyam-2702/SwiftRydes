Q: Why should I use this app?
A: Unicorns are faster, safer, and more reliable. Their population has grown significantly, making mass transportation by unicorns both feasible and affordable.
Q: How do you recruit the unicorns? How can I know that my unicorn is trustworthy?
A: Our unicorns are sourced from top-tier humane unicorn farms. They are grass-fed, free-range creatures raised on vegan, non-GMO diets. Unicorns also possess infallible morality and judgment, making them inherently trustworthy.
Q: How do I request a unicorn?
A: Download the Swift Rydes app, tap the button to request a unicorn, and one will arrive shortly.
Q: How much does it cost?
A: Prices vary based on distance and availability. You set your max price, and Swift Rydes matches you with a unicorn willing to accept that offer.
Q: How does it work?
A: Swift Rydes uses a powerful algorithm to match idle unicorns with ryders efficiently. Our serverless backend scales effortlessly to meet user demand.
Q: What if I have a complaint about my unicorn?
A: Swift Rydes is customer-obsessed. Our serverless chatbot support is available 24/7 to assist with any concerns.
Q: How do I cancel my ride?
A: Tap the “Cancel Ryde” button in the Swift Rydes app.
Q: Can I use Swift Rydes internationally?
A: Yes, in most countries except those restricted by international law and U.S. Treasury regulations.
Q: How do I pay for my ryde?
A: Add your payment method (credit card, debit card, Bitcoin wallet, or Vespene gas repository) in the app. You’ll be auto-charged after your ryde.
Q: How many passengers can my unicorn take?
A: Most unicorns carry one rider. Larger breeds can carry two, and the Sleigh version supports up to four passengers.
Q: What if I lose an item during my ryde?
A: Sadly, lost items that fall from a unicorn mid-ryde are nearly impossible to retrieve.
Q: How do I share my route information with someone else?
A: Use the “Share Route” button in the app to send your location and ETA to a contact.
Q: How do I rate my unicorn?
A: After your ryde, you can rate your unicorn in the app. Unicorns strive for 5-star feedback!
Q: What if my unicorn doesn’t match the photo in the app?
A: Do not board the unicorn. Report the imposter via the Swift Rydes app immediately.
Q: Can I use Concur with Swift Rydes?
A: Yes, connect your Concur profile to track business travel made via Swift Rydes.
Q: Can I request a specific unicorn?
A: You can’t select a specific unicorn, but you may choose type and size through the app.
Q: Why do you charge a service fee?
A: The service fee helps us cover operational costs and the care of our unicorn herd.
